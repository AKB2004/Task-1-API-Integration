### How to Take Screenshots:

#### Windows:
- **Full screen**: Press `PrtScn` or `Windows + Shift + S`
- **Selected area**: `Windows + Shift + S`

#### Mac:
- **Full screen**: `Command + Shift + 3`
- **Selected area**: `Command + Shift + 4`

#### Chrome DevTools (for mobile view):
1. Press `F12` to open DevTools
2. Click device toggle icon (or `Ctrl+Shift+M`)
